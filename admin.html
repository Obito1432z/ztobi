About Official Guilds

Official Guilds are featured prominently on the homepage and guild pages. They receive a special 👑 Official badge. Only administrators can mark or unmark guilds as official.

  • Official guilds appear first in guild listings
  • They get priority visibility on the homepage
  • The "Official" badge is shown on guild cards
  • You can have multiple official guilds
