Section 1

Introduction

At AniVerse, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

By using AniVerse, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us through our Contact page.

Section 2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, use our services, or interact with the community.

  • Account Data: Email address, username, display name, and password (encrypted).
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, banner image, bio, location, and preferences.
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, guild activity, and shared media.
  • Messages: Direct messages and chat history within the platform.
  • Guild Activity: Guild memberships, roles, and interactions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze usage.

All data is collected and processed in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Section 3

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, chosen username, and display name. Your password is encrypted and securely stored.

We use this information to authenticate your identity, communicate with you, and personalize your experience on AniVerse.

Section 4

Profile Information

You may choose to provide additional information such as a profile picture, banner image, bio, and location. This information is visible to other users and helps build the community.

You can update or remove your profile information at any time through your account settings.

Section 5

Community Content

Content you post, such as messages, comments, images, and guild activity, is stored and displayed publicly within the community. Please be mindful of what you choose to share.

You retain ownership of your content, but by posting it, you grant us permission to display and distribute it on AniVerse.

Section 6

Messages

Direct messages sent through AniVerse are stored securely and are visible only to the participants of the conversation.

We do not read your private messages. Message data is used to provide the messaging service and may be stored for a limited period.

Section 7

Guild Activity

Your guild memberships, roles, and interactions within guilds are stored and displayed to other guild members. This helps facilitate community engagement.

Guild leaders may have access to guild member information for moderation purposes.

Section 8

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience, analyze usage patterns, and remember your preferences.

We may use analytics tools to understand how our platform is used, which helps us improve and optimize the service.

Section 9

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve AniVerse, communicate with you, personalize content, and ensure a safe community environment.

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide community features like guilds, friends, and messaging.
  • To send notifications and updates relevant to your activity.
  • To analyze usage and improve our services.
  • To enforce our terms and protect the community.
Section 10

Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel and is strictly controlled.

  • Encrypted Authentication: Passwords are hashed and encrypted.
  • Secure Database: Data is stored in a protected environment.
  • Protected API: All API requests are encrypted and authenticated.
  • Role-Based Permissions: Access is controlled by user roles.
  • Session Management: Sessions are managed securely with expiration.
Section 11

Third-Party Services

AniVerse may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These may include:

  • Supabase: Our primary backend database and authentication service.
  • Anime APIs: External APIs for anime data (future integration).
  • Analytics Tools: To track usage and improve our platform.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them for more information.

Section 12

User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time.

  • You can view and edit your account information in your profile settings.
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • You can delete your account through account settings.
  • You can manage your privacy preferences in your settings.

Section 13

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time. When you delete your account, we will permanently remove your personal data from our systems.

Please note that some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes. Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe.

Section 14

Children's Privacy

AniVerse is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it.

Users between the ages of 13 and 18 must have parental consent to use the platform.

Section 15

International Users

AniVerse is accessible worldwide. By using our platform, you consent to the transfer of your data to countries where we operate.

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR for users in the European Union and the CCPA for California residents.

Section 16

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through announcements or email.

Continued use of AniVerse after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy. Please review this page periodically for updates.

Section 17

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through our Contact page.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and will respond to inquiries as quickly as possible.
